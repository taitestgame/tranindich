Dưới đây là dữ liệu phần Reading (Part 5, 6, 7) từ bài TOEIC Full Test 2 đã được lọc sạch, định dạng theo yêu cầu:

PART 5
Câu 101: It is .......... for all passengers to remain seated until the aircraft has come to a complete stop.
A. compulsory
B. compel
C. compulsion
D. compulsive
Đáp án: A

Câu 102: The new manager has a reputation for being .......... and fair to all employees.
A. decide
B. decision
C. decisive
D. decisively
Đáp án: C

Câu 103: Ms. Arnot was .......... surprised by the high quality of the entries in the competition.
A. pleasant
B. pleasure
C. pleased
D. pleasantly
Đáp án: D

Câu 104: The marketing team is working .......... to finish the project before the deadline.
A. hard
B. hardly
C. hardness
D. hardening
Đáp án: A

Câu 105: The proposal was .......... by the board of directors after a lengthy discussion.
A. accept
B. accepted
C. accepting
D. accepts
Đáp án: B

Câu 106: The company decided to .......... its operations into the European market.
A. expand
B. expansion
C. expansive
D. expanded
Đáp án: A

Câu 107: .......... the bad weather, the outdoor concert was a great success.
A. Although
B. Despite
C. Even though
D. However
Đáp án: B

Câu 108: The technician checked the equipment .......... to ensure it was functioning properly.
A. care
B. careful
C. carefully
D. caring
Đáp án: C

Câu 109: If the shipment .......... by tomorrow, please contact the supplier immediately.
A. doesn't arrive
B. won't arrive
C. hasn't arrived
D. didn't arrive
Đáp án: A

Câu 110: The CEO gave a .......... speech at the annual general meeting.
A. motivate
B. motivation
C. motivational
D. motivated
Đáp án: C
