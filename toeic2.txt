Dưới đây là dữ liệu phần Reading (Part 5, 6, 7) từ bài TOEIC Full Test 3 đã được lọc sạch và định dạng theo yêu cầu của bạn:

PART 5
Câu 101: The marketing director decided to .......... the launch of the new product until next month.
A. postpone
B. postponement
C. postponed
D. postponing
Đáp án: A

Câu 102: All visitors are required to show a valid ID .......... entering the government building.
A. during
B. before
C. among
D. within
Đáp án: B

Câu 103: The new software is .......... compatible with the existing operating system.
A. full
B. fully
C. fullness
D. filling
Đáp án: B

Câu 104: Mr. Tanaka was .......... for the successful completion of the international project.
A. praise
B. praised
C. praising
D. praises
Đáp án: B

Câu 105: Please review the attached document .......... and provide your feedback by Friday.
A. careful
B. care
C. carefully
D. caring
Đáp án: C

Câu 106: The company offers a generous .......... package to attract top talent in the industry.
A. benefit
B. beneficial
C. beneficiary
D. benefited
Đáp án: A

Câu 107: Although the research was .......... , the results were not conclusive.
A. extend
B. extensive
C. extensively
D. extension
Đáp án: B

Câu 108: The production manager is looking for ways to reduce .......... costs without sacrificing quality.
A. operate
B. operation
C. operational
D. operatively
Đáp án: C

Câu 109: If you have any questions .......... the new policy, please attend the orientation session.
A. regarding
B. regard
C. regardless
D. regarded
Đáp án: A

Câu 110: The renovation of the lobby will be .......... by the end of this week.
A. complete
B. completion
C. completed
D. completely
Đáp án: C

Câu 153: What is the purpose of the notice?
A. To announce a holiday schedule
B. To inform about a water shut-off

Câu 154: When will the service be interrupted?
A. Monday morning
B. Wednesday afternoon
C. Thursday evening
D. Friday night

B. Store enough water in advance
C. Call the manager immediately

Đáp án: B

[Questions 191-195]
Câu 191: What is the main subject of the first email?
A. A request for a refund
B. An inquiry about a venue

A. October 5th

C. November 15th

A. Due to a weekend surcharge
B. Because of the catering choice
